The 800sqm site, although just 50m from the water’s edge, was bordered on three sides by what was effectively a car park and was itself little more than a giant concrete slab, meaning our first challenge was how to combine a secure, budget-conscious perimeter fence with a degree of privacy for those using the future facility Our chosen solution was a series of 4x4 planks of wood finished with a deep varnish, positioned at 10cm intervals around the perimeter, sitting in a solid trough embedded beneath the concrete, providing cover to just above head height and ensuring the gaps were not large enough to pass through.
