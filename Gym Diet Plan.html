Best diet plan for fitness goal

Week 1: Phase 1: Cardio Focus

Monday to Friday: 15 minutes of light cardio activity followed by a 10-minute high-intensity interval training (HIIT) session.Saturday and Sunday: Rest days, during which you should not exercise.

Week 2: Phase 2: Full-Body Focus

Monday to Thursday: Full-body workout focusing on compound exercises and heavy resistance training. 30 minutes of high-intensity interval training is added after the full-body workout. Friday: Rest day. Saturday and Sunday: 30 minutes of full-body workout, with no added HIIT.

Week 3: Phase 3: Active Recovery

Monday to Thursday: Modified full-body workout that emphasizes lighter weights and fewer reps to allow for active recovery. Friday: 1 hour of slow-paced cardio. Saturday and Sunday: 30 minutes of light cardio activity, with a 10-minute cool-down.

Week 4: Repeat Phase 1 to 3

After successfully completing each phase, you should repeat the cycle of Phase 1 to 3 to maintain your progress.
